Our passion

Welcome! I’m Alejandra López, a linguist, teacher, and founder of Grama Spanish. This project was born from my passion for teaching Spanish to people from different places and cultures. At our school, we don’t follow a single methodology because we believe there isn’t just one way to learn. That’s why we adapt to each student’s needs, interests, and learning style, ensuring a dynamic, effective, and motivating experience. Whether you want to improve your Spanish for travel, work, or simply to enjoy the language, we are here to guide you with classes designed especially for you. Learning Spanish can be a unique and enriching experience! Are you ready to start with us?

Our Methodology

Personalized

Each student is different, with unique interests, goals, and ways of learning. That’s why our approach starts with getting to know you: we assess your needs and design a learning plan tailored to you.

Flexible

We don’t sell an innovative methodology as the only solution because we understand that student diversity requires flexibility. We combine modern tools with traditional methods to make the most of each approach.

Student-Centered

Learning revolves around you. We adapt to your pace, your goals, and your learning style so you can progress effectively and enjoy the process. Learning Spanish shouldn’t be standardized—it should be personalized.
