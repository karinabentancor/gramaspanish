BASIC SPANISH

Start from Scratch! Basic Spanish Course

This course is ideal for those with no prior knowledge of Spanish. You’ll learn how to communicate in everyday situations and build simple sentences with confidence.

What will you learn?

  • Introducing yourself and greeting others
  • Numbers, days, and months
  • Ordering food, asking for directions and help
  • Forming basic sentences in the present tense
  • Essential day-to-day vocabulary

Who is this class for?

Absolute beginners or those who want to reinforce the basics from the ground up.

Format

  • Weekly online classes
  • Access to interactive material
  • Constant speaking practice
